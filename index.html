FAQ

Questions we get a lot

What file formats do you accept? ▼

We accept STL, OBJ, 3MF, STEP and FBX files. STL is the most common for 3D printing. If you have a design in another format like Fusion 360, SolidWorks or Blender, just share the native file and we'll work with it. No file? No problem — just describe your idea and we can model it for you.

How long does delivery take? ▼

Most standard orders are printed and shipped within 2–3 business days. Complex or large prints may take 4–5 days. Pan-India shipping typically adds 2–4 days. International orders (USA, Canada, UK) usually take 7–14 days via tracked courier. Urgent orders can be prioritised — just ask us.

What materials are available? ▼

We offer PLA (great for gifts and décor), PETG (flexible and tough), ABS (heat resistant), ASA (weather resistant), Nylon, and various Resin types (standard and high-detail). Each material has different properties suited to different applications. Tell us what your print needs to do and we'll recommend the right material.

Can I order in bulk? ▼

Yes! We handle bulk orders for businesses, events, and gifting. Bulk orders (10+ pieces) often get better pricing per unit. Send us details about your quantity and requirements on Instagram and we'll give you a custom bulk quote.

How do I pay? ▼

For Indian customers: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, or cash on local pickup. For international orders: we accept international bank transfers and card payments via Razorpay. All transactions are fully secure. We typically ask for 50% advance and 50% before shipping.

I don't have a 3D file. Can you still help me? ▼

Absolutely! Just describe your idea, share a sketch, or send a reference photo. We can create the 3D model for you at a small additional charge, or help you find a suitable existing design. Just DM us on Instagram — no idea is too vague to start a conversation.
